Let's start with some practice trials. First you will see two objects appear on the screen, and a speaker will name one of the objects. After the speaker names the object, you will be prompted to type the word you just heard.

You will then see two more objects; again a speaker will name one of them, and you will be prompted to type what you just heard.

Finally, you will see three objects appear on the screen. The speaker will ask you to find one of the objects. Please select the object that the speaker names by clicking on it. The task will automatically proceed to the next trial.
